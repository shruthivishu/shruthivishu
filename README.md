### 🍓 about me

I'm **Shruthi Viswanathan**, a Design Innovation master's student who gets quietly obsessed with how people think, feel, and move through everyday spaces.

I began in engineering, asked too many *why*s, and eventually found myself in design. These days I turn messy questions into clear, human experiences: research, systems thinking, UI, and a bit of playful experimentation when the idea wants to breathe.

I like spaces that feel less like a showcase and more like a glimpse into how a mind works.

Right now I'm at **Maynooth University**, building things that hopefully make someone's day a little easier, or at least a little clearer.
